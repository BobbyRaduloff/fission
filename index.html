FISSION@ACS 2020

Deadline: January 31, 2020

What is FISSION?

FISSION is an international science fair organized by students for students.

Where and when?

March 27-28th, 2020
American College of Sofia

Why?

FISSION is the place to meet like-minded individuals, share knowledge and ideas with young scientists from all over Europe, have fun and get inspired!

What is our goal?

Our goal is to provoke students to take knowledge outside of the classroom. We want to challenge their creativity as they explore the practical applications of theoretical science. We also aim to unite students from different countries and scientific interests and create a community of ambitious young scientists.

How do I sign up?

Go to the signup page for further details. Make sure to read the guidelines before you apply. Keep in mind that the application form must be filled out by an advisor and that the deadline for filling it our is January 31, 2020.
