Welcome to FISSION 2020.

What is FISSION?

FISSION is an international non-profite science competition for high-schoolers. It is held annually at the American College of Sofia (ACS) in Bulgaria's capital, Sofia. It aims to encourage inquiry-based learning in students, and take them beyond the limitations of classroom science. FISSION is organized by a passionate of team ACS students.
